Apple2Mac Help

Apple2Mac emulates an enhanced 128k Apple //e computer with the following peripherals

  • Two 5.25" Disk ][ floppy disk drives (controller in slot 6)
  • Two Mockingboard sound cards in slot 4 & 5
  • 80 column card in slot 3

The Apple2Mac emulator software itself is Free Software, subject to the GNU General Public License version 2. Apple2Mac is copyright Aaron Culliney and other contributors. The source code is available from https://github.com/mauiaaron/apple2.

Apple2Mac relies on a number of free and open source software.

  • Portions of the sound and timing emulation were derived from AppleWin
  • Sound output depends on the OpenAL audio library
  • Disk image compression routines handled by zlib
  • Efficent C-language hashing with uthash
  • Unit testing by GreaTEST
